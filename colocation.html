Server Colocation – A Redundant and Robust Solution

Renaissance Colocation is a convenient, secure, and cost-effective solution for businesses that require a stable, high-performance network while maintaining complete control over their equipment and administration. Co-Locating your server in a data center saves you the costs of building your own network, and protects you from the hassles and instability of rented hosting. Our Data Center is carrier neutral, providing you with easy access to many carriers/networks with a network uptime SLA.

Multiple Redundant Connections to the Internet Backbone - Carrier Neutral

Servicing websites with high traffic volumes is an Renaissance specialty. We combine big pipes with multiple redundant high-speed connections to the Internet backbone. Our system automatically selects the fastest route to transport your data and redirects traffic to alternative carriers if a provider goes down.
